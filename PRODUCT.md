What this is: A text-based, TV-guide style website for Black film. No accounts, no hosting, no licensing. Just a scheduling mechanism that answers "what should I watch right now?" — filtered through Black culture broadly, not just the two channels Pluto gives us.
Scheduling: 2-hour fixed slots. Daily seed based on date so the schedule is deterministic and consistent for all users without a database. Loops the pool when exhausted, reshuffles, repeats until midnight. Resets at 12am.

What you see: Channel name, channel description, now playing, up next. That's it.
